/ˈɒnɪks/

Open Neural Network Exchange

<https://github.com/microsoft/onnxruntime>

ONNX was originally named Toffee[4] and was developed by the PyTorch team at Facebook.[5] In September 2017 it was renamed to ONNX and announced by Facebook and Microsoft. Later, IBM, Huawei, Intel, AMD, Arm and Qualcomm announced support for the initiative.
